Hatyaiwittayalai School
Hatyaiwittayalai School, founded in 1912 in Songkhla Province, is the oldest and most prestigious secondary school in southern Thailand. Known for its strong academic reputation and wide range of programs in science, mathematics, languages, and the arts, the school provides students with a solid foundation for higher education and future careers. With its emphasis on academic excellence, discipline, and community service, Hatyaiwittayalai has produced generations of outstanding graduates who have contributed greatly to the development of both the southern region and the nation as a whole.
