Universities

Thailand’s universities provide high-quality education across diverse fields such as science, technology, medicine, social sciences, and the arts. Leading institutions like Chulalongkorn, Mahidol, and Thammasat are known for academic excellence, research, and international partnerships. Regional universities like Chiang Mai and Khon Kaen also play key roles in local development and innovation, making Thailand a growing center for higher education in Southeast Asia.
