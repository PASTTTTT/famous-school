Bunyawat Witthayalai School
Bunyawat Witthayalai School, founded in 1905 in Lampang Province, is one of the oldest and most prestigious secondary schools in northern Thailand. Recognized for its academic excellence and strong traditions, the school offers a wide range of programs in science, mathematics, arts, and languages, while also promoting moral values, leadership, and community responsibility. With a long history of producing outstanding graduates who go on to play important roles in education, government, and professional fields, Bunyawat Witthayalai continues to serve as a leading educational institution dedicated to nurturing well-rounded and capable students.
