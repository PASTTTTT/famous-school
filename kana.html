Kanaratbumrung Pathumthani School
Kanaratbumrung Pathumthani School, established in 1972 in Pathum Thani Province, is a leading secondary school recognized for its strong academic programs and commitment to student development. Offering a wide range of studies in science, mathematics, technology, arts, and languages, the school focuses on equipping students with knowledge, skills, and moral values to prepare them for higher education and future careers. With its emphasis on academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement, Kanaratbumrung Pathumthani continues to play an important role in shaping capable and responsible young leaders for society.
