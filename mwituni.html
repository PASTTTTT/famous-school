Mahidol Wittayanusorn University

Mahidol University, established in 1943 in Bangkok, is one of Thailand’s most prestigious universities, renowned for its excellence in medicine, science, health sciences, and research. With a strong emphasis on academic rigor, innovation, and international collaboration, Mahidol University offers a wide range of programs in science, engineering, social sciences, and the arts. The university is dedicated to producing graduates who are leaders in their fields, contributing to Thailand’s development and addressing global challenges through education, research, and sustainable practices.
